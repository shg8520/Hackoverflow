About

Learn More About Us

We mainly focus on people's health during this tough and new situation that is the COVID-19. The pandemic has made people feel alone on their own. Even though the times are tough we must always make time to focus on our health both physical and mental.

Our main objectives while creating this applications are:

  • Being there for every human being
  • Help individual in understanding their health priorities
  • Enhance their mental and physical health

Health Guidance is committed to connecting people concerned about their health with Questionare. Our free questionare allows you to take a proactive approach to your wellness. The test is designed to provide anonymous and immediate feedback.
